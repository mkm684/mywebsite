Nokia

Kanta, Ontario
Jan 2018 - April 2018

Nokia Networks is a leader in the Telecommunications industry. At Nokia I worked in their R&D hardware lab developing next-generation routers. This gave me the opportunity to work closely with a team of highly dedicated designers and learn the ins-and-outs of high speed electronics design and embedded system architecture. Highlights include:

  • Worked on JIRA tickets on a daily basis.
  • Recreated and Fixed customer problems in Nokia’s Lab.
  • Developed my knowledge of Network Function Virtualization
  • Developed bash and TCL scripts to extract tech-support files from customers' servers.
